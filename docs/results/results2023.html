Results of the 1st International Knowledge Grapg Reasoning Challenge

Accepted papers

Submissions of the 1st International Knowledge Grapg Reasoning Challenge

We are pleased to announce that the winning works for the 1st International Knowledge Graph Reasoning Challenge (IKGRC 2023) have been selected through a rigorous judging process and an award ceremony was held. This year, we received a total of 6 submissions (2 in the Main Track, 2 in the Tool Track, and 2 in the Idea Track), and we would like to express our deepest gratitude to all the entrants. We would like to congratulate all the award winners. We would also like to express our sincere appreciation to all those who participated in the presentations and related events.
